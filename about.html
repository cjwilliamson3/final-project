The Mission:

Working with well known and well respected winegrape growers, the true stewards of our land, we source the finest fruits, from the most important vineyards in California.

Solitary Cellars Wine Company is based in Lodi, California and harvests fruit grown under the strict Lodi Rules for Sustainable Winegrowing Certification Program. As a member of the Lodi Winegrape Growers Association (LoCA) we are proud that Lodi has been named 2015 Wine Region of the Year by Wine Enthusiast Magazine.


Our philosophy towards wine making simple.

Seek the most productive and fertile regions, partner with the most dedicated and passionate growers, and take that passion and dedication to the winery to produce the finest wine possible.


Our philosophy towards business is simple.

Provide our customers an experience that delivers our wine in away that is educational and passionate. We take our customers on a journey through the vineyard and into the winery. The journey continues through the bottle and into the glass. The journey ends with a memory and a respect for the process that turns the finest fruit into the finest wines.
